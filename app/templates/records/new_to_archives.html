Getting started at The National Archives

If you are new to The National Archives and want some help getting started with your family history or any other kind of research, you're in the right place.

Use the sections below to find out where and how to starty your research or explore curatedhighlights from our collection.

What we have

The National Artchive is home to millions of historical documents, known as records, which were created and collected by UK central government departments and major courts of law.

For example, we have records from the Home Office, the Foreign Office and the Central Criminal Court.

It takes 185km of shelving to store our records, and this increases every year.
